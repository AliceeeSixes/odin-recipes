Description

A cured meat, mozarella, and salad sandwich


Ingredients

  • Bread (ideally sourdough or panini)
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Salami or other cured ham
  • Black pepper
  • Mayonnaise
  • Green leaf salad

Steps

  1. Slice the bread open, top with mozarella cheese, and grill until the cheese melts
  2. On top of the melted cheese add salad, mayonnaise, and ham
  3. Cack some black pepper onto the sandwich before closing and immediately eating
