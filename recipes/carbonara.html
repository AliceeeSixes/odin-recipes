Description

A creamy pasta dish made with egg and cheese


Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • One egg per person
  • Parmesan or pecorino cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Bacon, pancetta, or guanciale

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water
  2. Whilst the pasta is boiling, cook the bacon, pancetta, or guanciale in a separate pan
  3. Once the pasta had boiled, drain the water, setting some aside in a jug
  4. In a bowl, mix the egg, cheese, and some black pepper
  5. Over a low heat, eat the egg mix to the pasta and stir rapidly. The mixture should turn creamy
  6. If the sauce is too thick, add back in some of the saved pasta water
  7. Once the pasta sauce is of a consistency you like, add the ham and server immediately
