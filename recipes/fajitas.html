Description

A white British person tells you how to make Mexican food


Ingredients

  • Wraps
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Chicken
  • Pre-made Cajun spice mix/li>
  • Mayonnaise
  • Green leaf salad
  • Bell peppers
  • Salsa

Steps

  1. Use a griddle pan to fry the chicken in the seasoning powder
  2. Slice up the bell peppers and fry them in the same pan
  3. Assemble into a wrap with the remaining ingredients
